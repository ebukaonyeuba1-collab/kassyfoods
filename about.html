Our Story

More Than a Meal

Kassy Foods was born from a love of exceptional food, warm hospitality, and the belief that every dining experience should be memorable.

Born in Lagos, Built for the World

Kassy Foods began as a vision — to bring world-class fine dining to Lagos with a distinctly African soul. We believe that Nigerian ingredients, when treated with the same reverence as the finest European produce, can create extraordinary culinary experiences.

Our chefs draw inspiration from the bold flavours of West Africa and the refined techniques of classical French and modern European cuisine — resulting in dishes that are both familiar and surprising.

Every detail at Kassy Foods — from the curated wine list to the hand-selected tableware — reflects our commitment to excellence. We don't just serve food. We create memories.
